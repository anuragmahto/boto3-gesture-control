
Hands Combinations
-----------------------
[1,0,0,0,0] - thumb only
[1,1,0,0,0]	- thumb and index
[1,1,1,0,0]	- thumb, index, and middle

[0,1,0,0,0] - index only
[0,1,1,0,0] - index and mid
[0,1,1,1,0] - index, mid, and ring
[0,1,1,1,1] - index, mid, ring, & little
[0,0,0,0,1] - little only

[1,1,1,1,1] - All
